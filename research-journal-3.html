ACMI, The Lens

The Lens is an exhibit located towards the end of the ACMI gallery - Melbourne's museum of screen culture.
The space showcases a number of screens that allow guests to investigate the links between various pieces of online media such as movies, videos games etc.
Clicking the DNA strands floating about on the screen and you'll be taken on a journey of how the femme fatale relates to a number of other pieces of screen culture.
Displays like this would be edutainment - as not only are guests becoming more informed, but they are also having fun by interacting with the art piece.
