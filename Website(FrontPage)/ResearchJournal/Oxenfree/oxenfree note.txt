Oxenfree is a graphic adventure game developed by Night School Studio. Released in January 2016, it follows the story of Alex, a teenage girl on a weekend trip to a local island with friends. After supernatural events occur, Alex and her companions must unravel the island’s secrets. The game features a unique dialogue system where speech bubbles appear over Alex’s head, allowing players to choose between dialogue options while freely exploring the environment. It’s a captivating blend of mystery, exploration, and supernatural elements.
