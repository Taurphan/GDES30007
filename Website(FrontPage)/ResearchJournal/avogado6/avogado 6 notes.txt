Avogado6 is a Japanese illustrator and animator known for their unique art style. Their work often evokes deep emotions and thought-provoking themes. With their simple style and difficult subject matter, it creates a juxtaposition between the two creating an uncanny-valley of sorts.

Their art along with touching on concepts of relationships and the microcosm of the human, another core theme in their line of work is capturing the way our online lives affect our real life disposition.
